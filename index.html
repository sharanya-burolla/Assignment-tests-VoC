Chocolate Cake

Preparation time: 5 minutes
Servings: 8
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ¾ cups sugar
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  3. Combine dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  4. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Mix until smooth.
  5. Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin).
  6. Pour batter into prepared pans.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  8. Cool for 10 minutes, then remove from pans to wire racks.
  9. Frost as desired once completely cooled.
